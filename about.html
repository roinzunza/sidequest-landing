About

Building a better way
to hire locally.

SideQuest is a small, independent team rethinking what local hiring looks like in the age of swipe-and-tap. We believe local opportunity deserves better tools — not more friction.

Why we built this

Local hiring is broken.

The way local businesses hire today hasn't changed in 20 years. You write a job description nobody reads, pay $89 a month to a board nobody trusts, then sift through PDF resumes that say almost nothing about who someone actually is.

Meanwhile, the people who'd be great for those roles — the cooks, baristas, retail associates, line workers, contractors — are scrolling through job apps built for software engineers and corporate recruiters. They don't have a polished LinkedIn. They have a story to tell.

SideQuest exists because local jobs are not the same as corporate jobs. They shouldn't share the same pipeline.

What we believe

Three principles that
shape everything we build.

01

People over paper

A 30-second video tells a business more about a candidate than a 2-page resume ever could. We lead with the person.

02

Local means local

The most rewarding jobs are often a five-minute walk away. We surface opportunity by geography, not algorithm.

03

Fair, transparent pricing

Applicants never pay to apply. Businesses pay a flat rate that's a fraction of legacy job boards — no per-application surcharges, no surprise fees.
