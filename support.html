Support

Need a hand with SideQuest? We're here to help. Reach out anytime and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
Email Support dev@sidequestapp.io

We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

What to include in your message

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

  • A short description of the problem or question
  • Your account email (if different from the one you're writing from)
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The app version (found in Settings → About)
  • Screenshots or a screen recording, if applicable

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the app, tap Sign Up, and choose to continue with email, Google, or Apple. You'll then pick a role (applicant or business) and complete a short profile.

How do I switch between applicant and business roles?

Roles are tied to your profile. To change roles, head to Settings → Account, or contact support if you need help moving an existing account.

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your account email. We'll send a reset link. If you don't see it within a few minutes, check your spam folder.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account in the app. Your personal data will be deleted or anonymized within 30 days, as described in our Privacy Policy.

How do I report a bug or inappropriate content?

You can flag a listing or user directly from their profile by tapping the Report option. For bugs, email us at dev@sidequestapp.io with details and a screenshot if possible.

Is SideQuest free to use?

Yes. SideQuest is free for both applicants and businesses to use. Some optional features may be added over time, but the core experience will remain free.

Why don't I see jobs in my area?

SideQuest is currently rolling out city by city. If your area is light on listings, your account is still active and ready to go as soon as more local businesses join. Make sure your city and state are set correctly in your profile.

How does messaging work?

Once you apply to a gig (or an applicant applies to your listing), a chat thread opens automatically. You'll get a notification when there's a new message.
