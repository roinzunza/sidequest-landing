Mission

Our mission at SideQuest is to make job hunting fast, human, and local. We believe in opportunity without barriers—connecting real people to businesses through simple, resume-free applications.

About SideQuest

SideQuest helps you land jobs without the headache. Whether you’re a student, new grad, or just looking for extra income, SideQuest lets you showcase your personality, skills, and interests through quick profiles and short intro videos. Employers skip the cover letters and get straight to what matters—the real you.
