FAQ

Pricing questions.

Do I need to enter a card to try Premium?

No. Every new business account starts with a 30-day Premium trial automatically — no payment method required. When the trial ends, you'll be downgraded to Free unless you choose to subscribe.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Premium is month-to-month (or annual) with no contract. You can cancel directly from your iPhone's Subscriptions settings, and you'll keep Premium features until the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my listings if I downgrade?

If you have more than 1 active listing when you drop to Free, your existing listings stay visible but you won't be able to publish new ones until you're back under the limit. You can also use Extra Listing Slot ($9.99/month) to keep additional listings active without re-subscribing to Premium.

How does the annual plan work?

Annual billing is $199/year — roughly 33% off the monthly price (effectively 4 months free). It renews once a year and can be cancelled anytime from your phone's Subscription settings.

Are payments processed by Apple?

Yes. All subscriptions and add-ons go through Apple's App Store, so billing, receipts, refunds, and cancellations all live in your iPhone's Settings → Subscriptions.

Will pricing change later?

Possibly. As SideQuest grows, we may raise prices for new subscribers — but anyone already subscribed at a given price is locked in at that rate for the life of their subscription.

Is there a refund policy?

All refunds are handled by Apple, per their standard App Store refund policy. You can request a refund directly at reportaproblem.apple.com.
