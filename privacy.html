Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2025

SideQuest Labs LLC respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when using the SideQuest mobile application and related services.

By using SideQuest, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information such as:

  • Name, email, phone number, and password
  • Profile details including bio, skills, interests, city, and state
  • Profile photos
  • For business users: job listings and relevant business information
  • Messages and application interactions

We do not collect or store birthdays or precise dates of birth.

Third-Party Authentication

If you sign in using a third-party service such as Google or Apple, we may receive basic profile information (such as your name and email address) as permitted by your account settings with that provider. We do not receive or store your third-party account passwords.

Automatically Collected Information

We collect:

  • General usage data such as screen views and navigation behavior
  • Device information such as device type and operating system version
  • Basic log data such as IP address and error logs
  • General location data (city and state) based on your profile or device, used to show you local opportunities

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Help match job seekers and businesses
  • Improve app functionality
  • Communicate updates
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and related policies

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information with:

  • Other users when necessary for job listings or applications
  • Trusted third-party service providers such as hosting or analytics partners
  • Legal authorities when required or to protect rights and safety

4. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards, including encrypted token storage and secure HTTPS communication, to protect your information. However, no system can be guaranteed completely secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

6. Your Choices

  • You may edit or delete your profile at any time
  • You may disable non-essential notifications
  • You may request deletion of your data from our systems

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information

We do not sell your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dev@sidequestapp.io.

8. Children's Privacy

Users must be at least 18 years old to use SideQuest. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18. If such data is discovered, it will be promptly deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If changes are material, we will notify you in the app or by email. Continued use of the app after updates means you accept the revised policy.
